# 🚖 NYC Yellow Taxi Analytics – Power BI Dashboard

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing **NYC Yellow Taxi trip data**.  
The analysis focuses on **demand patterns, geographic mobility, pricing insights, and operational efficiency** using publicly available TLC taxi trip records.

The goal of the project is to transform raw transportation data into **actionable insights for urban mobility and fleet optimization**.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides insights into:

• Trip demand patterns across time  
• Geographic distribution of pickups and drop-offs  
• Revenue and pricing analysis  
• Trip duration and operational efficiency  
• Zone-level mobility flows

Key metrics analyzed include:

- Total Trips
- Total Revenue
- Average Fare
- Average Trip Distance
- Average Trip Duration
- Revenue per Minute
- Tip Distribution

---

# 📈 Key Insights

### 🚖 Overall Performance
- ~39K trips analyzed during January
- ~$1.39M total revenue generated
- Average fare around **$33.5**

### 📍 Demand & Geography
- **Manhattan and Brooklyn dominate demand**
- Certain zones consistently appear as top pickup locations
- Demand is concentrated in a small number of high-activity zones

### ⚖ Pickup vs Drop-off Balance
- Some zones act as **net exporters** (more pickups than drop-offs)
- Others behave as **net importers**, typically commercial hubs

### 💰 Revenue & Pricing
- **94% of revenue comes from base fares**
- Morning and Midday generate the highest total revenue
- Longer trips (60+ minutes) produce significantly higher fares

### ⏱ Efficiency & Duration
- Average trip duration is **~46 minutes**
- Efficiency varies throughout the day due to congestion
- Positive relationship between trip duration and revenue

# 🗂 Dataset

The data comes from **NYC TLC Yellow Taxi Trip Records**, which include fields such as:

- Pickup and Dropoff Datetime
- Passenger Count
- Trip Distance
- Pickup and Dropoff Zones
- Payment Type
- Fare Amount
- Tips, Tolls, Taxes, and Surcharges

These fields allow detailed analysis of **trip behavior, pricing, and mobility patterns**.
